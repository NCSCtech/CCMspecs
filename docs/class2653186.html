Attribute biom:ImageNativeScanningResolutionValue

A scanning resolution used by a specific biometric image capture device. Valid values between 19.29 and 99.99 OR equal to 00.00 (corresponds to 500ppi positive and negative 2% up to maximum allowed value.). Should only allow two decimal places

Attribute biom:ImageNominalTransmittingResolutionValue

A transaction's transmitting resolution, in pixels per mm. Valid value between 19.29 and 20.08 OR equal to 00.00 (corresponds to 500ppi positive and negative 2% ) OR between 38.58 and 40.16 (corresponds to 1000ppi positive and negative 2%.)
