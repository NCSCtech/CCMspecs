Attribute biom:FeatureLineColorValue

A fifth information item, line color / TLC, is an RGB color value expressed as a hexadecimal number. Some basic colors are red (FF0000), yellow (FFFF00), blue (0000FF), green (008000), black (000000) and white (FFFFFF). Leading zeros are required.

Attribute biom:FeatureLineThicknessValue

A sixth information item, line thickness / TLT, is expressed as a positive integer. Thickness is expressed in units of 0.01 mm Any value from 1 through 99 is acceptable; suggested values are 3 (thin); 6 (medium); 9 (thick), and 15 (bold).
